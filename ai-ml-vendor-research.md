# AI/ML Hiring Vendors Research Plan

## Target: Find AI/ML hiring vendors and HR/hiring manager contacts

### Research Approach:

1. **LinkedIn Search Strategy:**
   - Search for "AI ML hiring manager" + company names
   - Look for "Technical Recruiter AI" profiles
   - Target companies like: Accenture, Cognizant, TCS, Infosys, Wipro
   - Search for startups and mid-size companies hiring AI/ML roles

2. **Job Portals to Monitor:**
   - Indeed: "AI engineer" "ML engineer" remote/contract
   - LinkedIn Jobs: Filter by C2C, Contract, Remote
   - ZipRecruiter: AI/ML contract positions
   - AngelList: For startup AI/ML roles
   - Glassdoor: Company research + contact info

3. **Prime Vendors to Target:**
   - Kelly Services
   - Robert Half Technology  
   - Randstad Technologies
   - Modis (Adecco Group)
   - TEKsystems
   - Insight Global
   - CyberSeek
   - Apex Systems

4. **Research Focus Areas:**
   - Companies with 100-5000 employees (avoid massive corps, avoid tiny shops)
   - Recent AI/ML job postings (last 30 days)
   - Companies mentioning "hard to fill" or "specialized skills"
   - Remote-friendly positions
   - C2C/1099 friendly companies

5. **Contact Information to Collect:**
   - HR Manager name + LinkedIn profile
   - Hiring Manager name + LinkedIn profile  
   - Company email format (firstname.lastname@company.com)
   - Direct phone numbers when available
   - Preferred contact method

6. **Tools to Use:**
   - LinkedIn Sales Navigator (if available)
   - Company websites career pages
   - Crunchbase for startup info
   - Hunter.io for email formats
   - ZoomInfo/Apollo for contact details
